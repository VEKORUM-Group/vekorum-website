Accessibility

VEKORUM is committed to providing a website that can be used by as many people as possible, including people who use assistive technologies.

Measures taken

  • Semantic headings, landmarks, and descriptive page titles
  • Keyboard-accessible navigation, forms, and controls
  • Visible focus indicators and skip navigation
  • Text alternatives for meaningful imagery
  • Reduced-motion support
  • Responsive layouts and scalable text
  • Colour and contrast checks for core interface elements

Target standard and review status

We aim to conform to WCAG 2.2 Level AA. Automated structural checks have been completed, but a formal WCAG conformance assessment and representative assistive-technology testing have not yet been completed. We will update this statement as further testing and remediation are completed.

Known limitations

Animated visual elements are decorative and are disabled when a visitor has enabled reduced-motion preferences. If any content or interaction remains difficult to use, please contact us so that we can investigate and provide an accessible alternative.

Feedback

If you experience a barrier, email info@vekorum.com and identify the page, the problem, and any assistive technology used where you are comfortable doing so. We will work to provide the information in an accessible format and address the underlying issue where reasonably possible.
