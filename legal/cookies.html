Cookie policy

This website does not currently use advertising cookies or optional analytics cookies.

Current technology

The core website does not require advertising or analytics cookies. If optional analytics are configured in the future, the site may use local browser storage under the name vekorum-consent to remember whether a visitor accepted analytics or selected essential-only use. This preference is not used for advertising.

Hosting and essential operation

Our hosting and security providers may use strictly necessary technical information to deliver pages, protect the service, prevent abuse, and maintain network security. These technologies are not used by VEKORUM for behavioural advertising.

Optional analytics

Optional analytics are currently disabled. They will not be enabled until this policy, the privacy notice, and the website's consent controls have been updated and activated. Refusing optional analytics will not prevent access to the website.

Your controls

You can block or clear cookies and browser storage through your browser settings. Clearing storage may remove a saved preference and cause the website to request it again if optional analytics are later introduced.

Changes and contact

We may update this policy when website technology changes. The date above identifies the latest revision. Questions may be sent to info@vekorum.com.
