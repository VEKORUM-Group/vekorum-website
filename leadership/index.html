About our founder

VEKORUM was founded by a project management and management consulting professional with multidisciplinary experience spanning capital project delivery, infrastructure development, hyperscale data centre programmes, digital transformation, and supply chain management.

Ademola Adekemi, PMP

Founder and Managing Director

His experience combines project management, programme governance, infrastructure planning and delivery, operational improvement, organisational transformation, and business consulting, enabling him to bridge technical understanding with strategic business thinking while helping organisations navigate complexity with confidence.

Throughout his career, he has contributed to projects across the technology, infrastructure, transportation, commercial development, and public sectors. His experience includes supporting hyperscale data centre commissioning and programme delivery, contributing to the planning, design, and delivery of high-rise developments, stadium projects, transportation infrastructure, roadway design, road cross sections, and curb design, and participating in multidisciplinary stakeholder planning for a federal airport development.

His professional experience also includes supply chain management, where he contributed to inventory optimisation, vendor coordination, operational efficiency, and process improvement within complex operational environments.

Ademola is PMP certified and maintains active standing. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering from Covenant University and a Master's degree in Management Engineering from Audencia Business School. Combining technical expertise with strategic management, he founded VEKORUM to help organisations strengthen governance, improve delivery capability, and execute complex initiatives with confidence.

He believes consulting should do more than provide advice. It should strengthen organisations, develop lasting capability, and leave leaders with the confidence to deliver what matters most long after the engagement has ended.

How We Lead

Leadership is not an oversight function. It is an active responsibility.

VEKORUM engagements are designed to be led with accountability, sound judgement, and a commitment to leaving organisations stronger than we found them. Our role is not only to help deliver today's objectives but also to strengthen the leadership capability, governance, and delivery confidence that shape tomorrow's success.

  • Present

    Present when leadership matters most.

    We remain close to the work, engaged with delivery teams, and available when important decisions must be made.

  • Clear

    Communicate with honesty and precision.

    We communicate evidence, risk, priorities, and required actions with clarity, ensuring informed decisions are made with confidence.

  • Consistent

    Lead with integrity and discipline.

    Our decisions, actions, and delivery remain aligned with the objectives, governance, and standards established at the beginning of every engagement.

  • Transferable

    Leave organisations stronger than we found them.

    Our engagements are designed to strengthen governance, build confidence, and develop organisational capability that continues creating value long after our work is complete.
